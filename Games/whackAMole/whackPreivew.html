Whack a Mole

The game features a 3×3 grid with 9 holes. A single mole randomly appears in one of the holes, and the player must click it to score a point. A wrong click deducts one point. Occasionally, a joker plant appears instead of the mole; striking it ends the game immediately. The design emphasizes precision as well as speed, since careless clicks reduce the score or cause game over.

1. One mole appears randomly in any of the 9 holes.
2. Clicking the mole gives +1 point.
3. Clicking an empty hole deducts 1 point.
4. A joker plant may appear randomly.
5.Clicking the joker plant ends the game immediately..

This game is designed as a fun way to sharpen reflexes and improve focus. For elderly players, it helps keep the mind active and supports cognitive health. For children, it builds hand-eye coordination and quick thinking. Overall, it serves as an engaging brain exercise suitable for all ages.
