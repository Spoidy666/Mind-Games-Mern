Memory Tile

This version of Memory Tile challenges short-term visual memory. Two sets of tiles are shown: correct tiles marked with a star, and incorrect tiles in red. The player is given 3 seconds to memorize the correct tiles. When the game starts, they must click only the correct tiles to earn points. Clicking a wrong tile reduces the score. Precision and focus are essential.

Hardness level     ★ ★ ★ ★ ★

1. Two sets of tiles are shown briefly for 3 seconds.
2. Correct tiles are marked with a star; incorrect tiles are red.
3. Click the correct tiles once the game starts to earn 1 point per tile.
4. Clicking an incorrect tile reduces 1 point.
5. The player must recall the correct positions quickly and accurately.

This game is designed to improve short-term visual memory, attention to detail, and reflexes. It helps elderly players maintain cognitive sharpness and allows children to strengthen focus and memory recall. Quick thinking and precision are encouraged, making it a stimulating and engaging brain exercise.
